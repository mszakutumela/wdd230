Member Levels and Benefits

Non-Profit Membership (it's free)

- Receive all benefits the Chamber has to offer, free of charge.

Bronze Membership (it's $100)

- Get access to events and training hosted by the Chamber.

Silver Membership (it's $200)

- Receive the same benefit as a Bronze Member.
- Receive 50% discount at Planet Fitness Gym.
- Get three days advert space on our homepage for your business.

Gold Membership (it's $400)

- Receive the same benefit as a Bronze and Silver Member.
- Get free approval to host events.
- Get free access to VIP lounges at all events.
