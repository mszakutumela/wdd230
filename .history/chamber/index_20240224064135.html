Welcome to Tshwane Chamber of Commerce

The chamber operates in the city of Tshwane (Pretoria), South Africa. he jacaranda tree is one of the major symbols of the city. Jacaranda trees are native to South America and first came to Tshwane (Pretoria) in 1888. A travelling nurseryman called Templeman sold two Jacaranda seedlings to Jacob Cilliers, who resided at Myrtle Lodge in Tshwane (Pretoria). A plaque was also placed at the site to commemmorate the event. The plaque was unveiled on, 8 November 1939. Recent News

Storm leaves residents without water and homeless

At least nine areas in Region 7, including Thembisile Hani Local Municipality, might feel the pinch of water shortage after their local water treatment plant was struck by lightning during the weekend storm. Meanwhile, Tshwane Emergency Services spokesperson Charles Mabaso reported that multiple shacks and houses were blown away and trees were uprooted by a storm that ripped through parts of the municipality on Tuesday afternoon and evening. Many shacks in Cullinan and Refilwe Extension 1, 2 and 4 were blown away by the storm, but no one was injured.
